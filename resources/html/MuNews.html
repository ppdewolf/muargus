18 May 2005: The suppression procedure in combination with the household risk gave undesirable results.
Argus only suppressed records where the BIR > threshold/hhsize.
New insights have resulted in the following procedure:
Only records for which BIR>threshold/hhsize and BHR>threshold are suppressed now.
In these formulas is BIR the BaseIndividualRisk and BHR the BaseHouseholdRisk.
